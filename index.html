About

A quick bit about me.

I am a data engineer who enjoys working across the end-to-end data journey.

My main specialty is the modern data tech stack: dbt, DataBricks, Snowflake and AWS/Azure/GCP.

I have over 2 years experience at IBM, working as both a data engineer and data scientist, I currently work at DataCamp as an expert on governance in data and AI and I hold a MSc Data Science from the University of Manchester. I am also certified in Azure and dbt.

This page is a way for me to show everything I've done and share my work with the world. Feel free to get in touch with thoughts, feedback or just to say hello.
